Knowledge Base Articles

Overview

Here are samples of knowledge base articles that I wrote for Dell EMC Isilon and Microsoft.

Knowledge base articles come in two types: break/fix and how-to.

A break/fix article provides information about an issue that customers might have experienced, and provides a workaround or resolution for the problem. The typical structure for a break/fix article is Issue, Cause, and Workaround or Solution.

A how-to article is a procedure that explains how to do a task not found in the product documentation. How-to articles supplement product guides by providing special-case or custom instructions.
