Academic Research

Download the journal article “Expert Yet Vulnerable: Understanding the Needs of Transit-Dependent Riders to Inform Policy and Design”

Overview

When I was a graduate student at the University of Washington, I assisted in an ethnographic research study. For one semester, my teammates and I looked at how transit-dependent populations use the King County Metro Transit system, and how vulnerable communities, especially the homeless, overcome barriers to ridership. We identified several systemic issues and proposed human-centered design solutions to make the transit system more fair and inclusive.
